How to Setup (part 1) - BootNTR & InputProc Installation

On your 3DS:
  • Download/Install "InputProc" and "BootNTR Selector" from titleDB in FBI
  • Open titleDB

  • Press Right on the DPAD Three Times

  • Press A on BootNTR Selector

  • Press A again on Install

  • Press A to Install BootNTR Selector

  • Press Any Button to Continue

Repeat the same Steps for Installing InputProc


Getting Your 3DS IP Address
  • Tap Remote Install in FBI

  • Tap Receive URLs over the network

  • Copy your 3DS IP Address

  • Press B Twice
  • Press Start to Exit FBI
  • Unwrap InputProc & BootNTR Selector

Notes: (won't be relevant untill after finishing the tutorial)

Everytime you Reboot your 3DS you'll have to do the following Steps:

(Before opening kit-kat-touch on your PC)

  • Open InputProc -> wait until its back on the homemenu
  • Open BootNTR Selector -> Wait until its back to the Home menu

If you can't get the PC to Connect or you've already been Streaming to the PC you might have to:
  • Restart your 3DS
  • Repeat the Above Steps
  • Attempt to connect with kit-kat-touch.exe again
