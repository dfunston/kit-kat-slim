Setup & Use Rosalina Gamepad InputRedirectionClient-Qt (Luma v8.0)

On your 3DS:

  • Press "L+Select+Down" to Open the Rosalina Menu

  • Scroll down & Hit A on "Miscellaneous"

  • Press A on "Start InputRedirection"

  • Press B - 3 Times to Exit Rosalina

On your PC:

  • Connect your XInput Gamepad to your PC (Xbox One, 360)
  • Download kit-kat-touch.exe Here
  • Download InputRedirectionClient-Qt.zip Here or Inside kit-kat-touch (second Tab)
  • Extract the InputRedirectionClient-Qt.zip contents to the kit-kat-touch.exe Directory

Make Sure InputRedirectionClient-Qt.exe is in the same Folder as kit-kat-touch.exe

  • Open kit-kat-touch.exe

  • Press "Enable Rosalina Gamepad"

  • Enter in your 3DS IP Address into the "InputRedirectionClient-Qt Window" that appears

Your Gamepad should now be working on your 3DS

Do Not to Click "Power (long)" while playing or you'll Exit the Game WITHOUT SAVING

If you want to Video Stream + Use Rosalina Gamepad

  • Load BootNTRSelector on your 3DS

  • Download "NTRViewer_Starter_Kit.zip" - Inisde kit-kat-touch.exe (second tab)

  • Extract "NTRViewer Folder" from NTRViewer_Starter_Kit.zip & Place it in kit-kat-touch.exe Directory

  • Enter 3DS IP Address into the top of kit-kat-touch.exe (main tab)

  • Click the "CONNECT" Button in kit-kat-touch.exe


Make sure you Enable Rosalina Gamepad Before trying to Connect or NTRViewer Will Crash
